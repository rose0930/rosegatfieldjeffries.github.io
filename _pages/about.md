**About**
I am a PhD student in History and Philosophy of Science at University of Pittsburgh.

I work on philosophy of science and AI, especially epistemological and ethical questions that arise when AI and automation are integrated into scientific practice.

Before studying at Pittsburgh, I completed an MPhil in the History and Philosophy of Science, Medicine, and Technology at University of Cambridge. Before that I studied History and Philosophy of Science and Political Economy at University of Sydney. I was born and raised in Whangaparāroa, Aotearoa New Zealand.
