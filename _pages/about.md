# Rose Gatfield-Jeffries
 
I am a PhD student in [History and Philosophy of Science at the University of Pittsburgh](https://www.hps.pitt.edu/). I work on philosophy of science and AI, especially epistemological and ethical questions that arise when AI and automation are integrated into scientific practice.
 
I am a Graduate Student Awardee at Pitt's [Hub for AI and Data Science Leadership (HAIL)](https://www.aihub.pitt.edu/) — you can read a short profile on my participation [here](https://www.aihub.pitt.edu/news/hail-graduate-awardees-spotlight-rose-gatfield-jeffries). In Summer 2026, I participated in Northeastern's [AI + Data Ethics (AIDE) Summer Program](https://cssh.northeastern.edu/ethics/aide-summer/).
 
I was born and raised in Aotearoa New Zealand.
 
Before Pittsburgh:

MPhil in History and Philosophy of Science, Medicine, and Technology at University of Cambridge
